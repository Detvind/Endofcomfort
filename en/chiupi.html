     CHIU PI is a versatile musician.
     Long exposed to music, painting, photography, theater, martial arts, dance and body movement training. Founded in Taipei in 2013, CHIU PI recorded and released 16 online music albums, 3 physical CD albums and a science fiction - End of Comfort.
     After join ROKON, CHIU PI change the style to eternal and spirituality. It focuses on the internal state of mind: remain fully open to possibilities.
     He creates avant-garde music that has an ethereal and introspective style, changing the general perception of electronic music. He evaluates the tensions between nature versus technology, culture versus economics, homogeneity versus pluralism, and re-imagines the future of our human experience in a fantastical, poetic, and magical backdrop.

     End of Comfort is the first novel written by CHUI PI himself.
     "The inspiration of End of Comfort is from ultimate source. Therefore, it will be effective in every era. It neither reveres any character nor cares about any opinion of it. It' s even impossible to change its connotation with the reader or the environment. I put all my love on it, just as the sun rises in the East."
         - CHIU PI
